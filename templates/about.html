About PlantGuard AI

An AI-powered system for early detection and diagnosis of diseases in perennial plants.

Go Home

Our mission

PlantGuard helps growers spot issues earlier so they can act sooner and waste less. It uses a trained deep learning model to analyze leaf images and identify diseases with high accuracy, providing quick and reliable results.

What PlantGuard can do

On-device by default

Images are processed locally in the demo. For deployment, swap in your model for full classification without sending data to a server.

Early alerts

Catch stress patterns quickly and respond before minor issues become yield-threatening problems.

Low-connectivity friendly

Runs in modern browsers with no network call—ideal for fields with spotty coverage.

How it works (at a glance)

Capture

Take a single-leaf photo in good light.

Analyze

The AI model computes disease classification and confidence.

Act

View predicted class and confidence, then log results or share with agronomy tools.

Backed by research

PlantGuard is inspired by strong results from CNNs and transfer learning for plant disease detection, plus evidence that earlier detection reduces crop loss and chemical use.

  • High accuracy from CNN/transfer-learning approaches.
  • Value of early detection for yield protection and smarter input use.
  • Focus on perennial crops such as coffee, tea, and cinnamon.

FAQ

Is my photo uploaded to a server?

No. In this demo, your image is processed locally and never leaves your device.

What does the demo model do?

The sample version uses a lightweight method. Swap this for your trained CNN model when deploying.

Can I keep a record of my scans?

Yes. The homepage includes a History section that stores results on your device using localStorage.
