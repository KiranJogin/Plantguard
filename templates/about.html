About PlantGuard AI

An AI-powered system for early detection and diagnosis of diseases in perennial plants.

Go Home

PlantGuard AI uses a trained deep learning model to analyze leaf images and identify diseases with high accuracy, providing farmers and gardeners with quick and reliable results.

Simply upload a photo of your plant leaf, and our AI will classify it into healthy or diseased categories, along with a confidence score.
